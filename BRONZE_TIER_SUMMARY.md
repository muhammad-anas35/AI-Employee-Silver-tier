## 🔄 COMPLETE WORKFLOW

```

2. Watcher Detects (automatic)
   └─> Creates: /Needs_Action/FILE_myfile_123.md

3. Claude Processes (manual or scheduled)
   └─> /process-vault-tasks skill

   └─> /Plans/PLAN_task_123.md
```
